对齐工具: https://montreal-forced-aligner.readthedocs.io/
github项目地址: https://github.com/ming024/FastSpeech2

命令行：
python3 synthesize.py --text "大家好" --speaker_id 212 --restore_step 900000 --mode single -p config/AISHELL3/preprocess.yaml -m config/AISHELL3/model.yaml -t config/AISHELL3/train.yaml

mfa train corpus_directory dictionary_path output_directory
